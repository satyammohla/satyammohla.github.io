Hi! I am a freshly minted researcher interested in AI Bias, AI for Social Good, Remote Sensing, AI Policy & affordable healthcare.

My master's thesis in AI and computer vision was advised by Subhasis Chaudhari and Biplab Banerjee, and was awarded the IIT Bombay Graduate Research Award (URA03) & nominated (ongoing) for INAE Student Innovator Award. In my early years, I developed low-cost biomedical devices for Na/K and Alb/Crea diagnostics, leading to many publications, patents and awards.

I recently graduated with a B.Tech-M.Tech in Electrical Engineering and a minor in IEOR from IIT Bombay. I have worked as a researcher at AI IMPACT Lab, AI Policy Lab, Vision & Image Processing Lab, NanoBios Lab and Sony R&D Japan. I have also spent a few months at NTU & NBS Singapore as an undergraduate Temasek TfLEaRN Scholar.

I'll be visiting Le Laboratoire d’imagerie, de vision et d’intelligence artificielle (LIVIA Lab) as a Shastri Fellow this Fall, before moving to Honda R&D Center X Akasaka, developing AI products & mobility services around autonomous vehicles of tomorrow.
